FAQ

Q: Is there a minimum order value for delivery?
A: Yes, a minimum order of $15 applies.
Q: Is there a delivery surcharge?
A: A surcharge of $4.00 applies for every order
made.

Q: Can the delivery surcharge be considered as
part of the minimum order amount?

A: No. The $15 minimum order amount is
limited to the food order. The delivery
surcharge is a separate charge.

Q: Can I place a large order online?

A: For orders above $300, please call our
hotline at 6235 3535.

Q: Can I place an order more than 7 days in advance?

A: For advance orders of more than 7 days,
please call our delivery hotline 6235 3535.
